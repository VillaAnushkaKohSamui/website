Beyond the Villa

Experiences

Curated for You

Everything arranged.
You just enjoy.

Our villa manager and team are here to help make every moment of your stay special. Whether it's a private chef dinner, an in-villa massage or a full-day yacht charter -
simply ask, and we'll take care of it.

Dining

Floating Breakfast

The most-photographed moment at Villa Anushka. A beautiful tray floated in the infinity pool, overflowing with tropical fruits, pastries and fresh juice - with Chaweng Bay in the background.

Dining

Private Chef Dinners

A table set just for your group, overlooking the sea. Our private chef creates bespoke menus for special occasions - from authentic Thai feasts to international cuisine and seafood BBQs.

Culture

Thai Cooking Classes

Learn to cook authentic Thai dishes in your own villa kitchen with a private instructor. Green curry, pad thai, mango sticky rice - a hands-on experience everyone loves.

Wellness

In-Villa Massages

Traditional Thai massages and aromatherapy brought directly to the villa. Unwind by the pool or on the terrace without going anywhere.

Island Life

Boat Trips & Yacht Charters

Explore the waters around Koh Samui - snorkelling at Koh Tan, visiting Ang Thong Marine Park, sunset cruises or full-day yacht charters.

Exploration

Island Tours & Excursions

Big Buddha, Grandfather and Grandmother Rocks, Elephant Sanctuary, waterfall hikes, temple tours - our team is happy to arrange the best of Koh Samui for your group.

Celebrations

Birthday & Anniversary Setups

Birthday cakes, flower arrangements, balloon setups, special table décor and surprise moments. We love making celebrations memorable - just tell us what you have in mind.

Arrival & Getting Around

Transfers & Car Hire

Airport transfers, private drivers, car and scooter hire arranged for your stay.

Convenience

Grocery Pre-Stocking & Babysitting

Arrive to a fully stocked villa - drinks, snacks, fresh produce all waiting for you (available on request). Babysitting services also available so the adults can enjoy a night out.

Let's Plan Your Stay

Tell us what would make your trip perfect

Get in touch and our team will help craft a bespoke experience for your group -
from arrival to the last sunset.
