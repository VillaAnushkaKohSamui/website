The Villa

Overview

700m² of contemporary luxury across three floors

Villa Anushka is a place where people come together. Perched on a hillside overlooking the sea, it sleeps up to 12 guests across six en-suite bedrooms and three spacious floors. There's plenty of room to enjoy time together, and just as much to find your own quiet corner.

The villa was thoughtfully designed with both families and groups of friends in mind. Floor-to-ceiling glass throughout keeps the ocean view constant - whether you're in bed or sinking into the sofa.

Top Floor

Open-plan living, dining, kitchen & bedroom

Step onto the top floor and the view hits you immediately. Expansive floor-to-ceiling sliding glass doors open fully onto the terrace and infinity pool, creating a seamless flow between indoors and out.

The dining table seats 12 - perfect for breakfasts, long lunches or special dinners prepared by a private chef. The kitchen is fully equipped with everything you need.

The top floor also has its own king-size bedroom with floor-to-ceiling sliding doors that open directly onto the pool terrace - waking up to that view is something else.

  • Dining for 12
  • Oven, fridge, freezer, dishwasher
  • Coffee maker, microwave, washing machine
  • Comfortable lounge with flat-screen TV
  • Sliding doors directly onto pool terrace
  • King-size bedroom with panoramic sea views & private terrace access
Middle Floor

Three sea-view bedrooms & the games room

Three spacious double bedrooms, each with mesmerising sea views. And then there's the games room - the place everyone gravitates to in the evenings. Pool table, darts, futsal, board games, TV corner. Adults and children love it in equal measure.

  • 3 king-size bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms
  • All rooms with panoramic sea views
  • Games room: pool table, darts, futsal table
  • TV corner and board games
  • Air-conditioning throughout
Lower Floor

Master suite, twin room & private lounge

The lower floor offers a generously sized master bedroom and a twin room, alongside a private lounge with enormous floor-to-ceiling windows. The ocean is right there when you wake up.

  • Master bedroom with en-suite
  • Twin bedroom with en-suite
  • Lounge with floor-to-ceiling windows
  • Exercise bike & dedicated work desk
The Infinity Pool

12m × 4m of pure horizon

The pool quickly becomes the heart of every stay. Hours pass without anyone noticing. Sun loungers line the terrace, a shaded sala offers cooler respite, and as the sun dips the light turns golden across Chaweng Bay.

Floating breakfast trays can be arranged for a truly memorable morning.

  • 12m × 4m infinity pool
  • Multiple sun loungers on terrace
  • Shaded sala for afternoon retreats
  • Floating breakfast available on request
