Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Villa Anushka Koh Samui ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the personal information of everyone who contacts us or enquires about a stay. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry via our contact form, we collect the information you provide, which may include:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Dates and details of your intended stay
  • Any additional information you choose to include in your message

If you book through a third-party platform (Airbnb, Booking.com, VRBO), your information is also subject to that platform's privacy policy. We receive only what is necessary to manage your booking.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Manage your booking and communicate about your stay
  • Arrange any additional services you have requested

We do not use your information for marketing purposes, and we do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except where necessary to fulfil your booking (for example, sharing arrival details with our housekeeping team).

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as is necessary to manage your booking and any follow-up communications. If your enquiry does not result in a booking, we will not retain your information beyond the enquiry exchange.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to request corrections, or to ask us to delete it. To make any such request, please contact us at info@villaanushkakohsamui.com.

Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies necessary for basic site functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party platforms including Airbnb and Booking.com. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these platforms and encourage you to review their policies directly.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please get in touch via our contact page.
