Villa Anushka

More than a villa.
A place to come together.

Over the years we've hosted family reunions, milestone birthdays, anniversaries, and special holidays with friends. What guests often tell us afterwards is that their favourite memories weren't the excursions or restaurants - it was simply spending time together at the villa.

Perched on a beautiful hilltop above Chaweng Noi, Villa Anushka sleeps up to 12 guests across six bedrooms and three floors. With breathtaking sea views, an infinity pool, games room and multiple living spaces, there's room to gather together and room to find your own quiet corner.
