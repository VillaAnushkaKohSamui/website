Location

Where We Are

Hilltop privacy,
with everything within reach

Villa Anushka enjoys an elevated hilltop position above Chaweng Noi - one of Koh Samui's most desirable coastal areas. The villa's elevated setting means cooling sea breezes, exceptional privacy and some of the most spectacular views on the island.

Chaweng Noi Beach is just a 2-minute drive away, and Koh Samui's restaurants, beach clubs and attractions are all within easy reach. While the hillside location means a car or scooter is recommended, our team can arrange transport throughout your stay.

Getting Here

✈️ By Air: Fly into Samui International Airport (USM) - just 15 minutes away. Bangkok Airways operates frequent direct flights from Bangkok, Hong Kong and beyond.

🚖 Airport Transfer: We arrange private airport transfers directly to the villa. Just share your flight details when booking.

⛴️ By Ferry: Koh Samui is easily reached by ferry from the mainland (Donsak Pier, Surat Thani) or from nearby islands including Koh Phangan. Ferries arrive at Nathon Pier or Bangrak Pier, both around 30 minutes from the villa.

🛵 Getting Around: We recommend hiring a car or scooters to explore the island. Our team arranges both.

About the Island

Koh Samui -
Thailand's luxury island

Koh Samui is the third largest island in Thailand and fast becoming one of the world's most sought-after holiday destinations. With outstanding natural beauty alongside a vibrant nightlife and an international restaurant scene, it's the ideal location for a luxury villa holiday.

From the turquoise waters of Chaweng and Lamai to the charming Fisherman's Village in Bophut, the island has something for everyone - and Villa Anushka puts you right in the heart of it.
