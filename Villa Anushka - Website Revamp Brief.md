## Floor by Floor

**Top Floor** — Open-plan living, dining and kitchen with floor-to-ceiling sliding glass doors opening onto the terrace and infinity pool. Dining for 12. Kitchen with oven, fridge, freezer, dishwasher, microwave, coffee maker, washing machine, dryer. Seamless indoor-outdoor flow.

**Middle Floor** — Three sea-view double bedrooms + enormous games room: pool table, darts, futsal table, board games, TV corner. The games room is consistently highlighted in guest reviews as a favourite for both adults and children.

**Lower Floor** — Master bedroom + twin room. Lounge with floor-to-ceiling windows and ocean views. Exercise bike. Dedicated desk. Plush seating.

**Outdoor** — Infinity pool with lilos and inflatables. Sun loungers. Shaded sala. Glass balustrade terrace — the sunset spot every guest remembers.

## Design Direction

**Aesthetic:** Boutique & personal — warm, intimate, editorial. Anya's story and care should come through.

**Colour palette (suggested):**
- Deep charcoal / near-black (from villa's dark interiors and logo)
- Warm sand / stone (exterior textures in photos)
- Turquoise / aqua (the ocean — a signature visual)
- Crisp white (clean luxury feel)

**Typography:** Elegant serif for headings (story, warmth), clean sans-serif for body
**Photography style:** The Jan 2023 HD shoot is the strongest — lifestyle + architecture. Lead with (86), (108), (16) as hero images.
**Video:** Use the reels as background video on homepage hero section

**Key UX improvements needed:**
- Current site is client-rendered Wix — consider whether to stay on Wix or move to a more editorial platform (Squarespace, Webflow)
- Add floating booking/inquiry button (persistent CTA)
- Mobile experience is currently poor — priority to fix
- Add Instagram feed widget (340 posts is strong social proof)
- Add Airbnb/Google review widget with live star rating
